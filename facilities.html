WALKING AREA

Enjoy a self-guided hotel-to-hotel walking holiday, with the satisfaction of walking from village to village.

With no group, you can walk at your own pace, carrying only what you need for the day – we arrange luggage transfers, and provide you with a map and our trusted route notes. What makes inn-to-inn walking so rewarding is the sense of journey.

Depending on the holiday you choose, you may cover over 100 kilometres during your time away. This is very fulfilling in itself, but is even more so given that you can often clearly see the landscapes changing gradually as you progress.

Inn-to-inn walking also gives you the opportunity to stay at a number of different hotels along the way, providing an added sense of variety
